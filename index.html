Digital Poster

By Group 7

This website presents the development and progress of our prototype entitled “Digital Poster for Improving Awareness on Slow Internet Connection.” Our group identified slow internet connection as a common issue in schools that affects students’ learning, research, online activities, and communication.

Through this project, we aim to inform students and school administrators about practical and realistic solutions that can help reduce connectivity problems without requiring expensive equipment or major infrastructure changes.

Project Overview

Our prototype focuses on designing an informative digital poster that explains the causes of slow internet connection and suggests strategic solutions such as proper router placement, managing connected devices, and minimizing network congestion.

As Grade 8 students, we understand that providing additional routers or upgrading internet service plans may not always be financially possible. Therefore, our solution emphasizes awareness, optimization, and proper usage.
